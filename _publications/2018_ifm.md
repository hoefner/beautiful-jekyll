We develop and implement a translation from the Algebra for Wireless Networks (AWN) into the milli Common Representation Language (mCRL2). As a consequence, the sophisticated verification and analysis toolset of mCRL2 becomes available for AWN specifications.

We show that the translation preserves strong bisimilarity, ensuring that safety properties can be automatically checked using the mCRL2 toolset. To demonstrate the usability of the translation, the paper reports on a representative case study.
