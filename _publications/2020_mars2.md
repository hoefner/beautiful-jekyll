We present three formal models of the OSPF routing protocol. The first two are formalised in the timed process algebra T-AWN, which is tailored for routing protocols and specifies protocols in pseudo-code that remains easily readable.
The two T-AWN models differ in their level of detail and abstraction. From the more abstract model, we derive a third model based on networks of timed automata. This model can be executed and analysed in the Uppaal model checker.
