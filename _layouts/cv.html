Summary

Dr. Peter Höfner is an Associate Professor at the Australian National University (ANU) and Associate Director of Education in the School of Computing. His research transforms how software systems are developed — ensuring their trustworthiness and security to the highest degree possible: the certainty of mathematical proof. He is a world leader in the formal analysis of protocols used by emergency services worldwide, and has led collaborative research with Australia's Defence Science and Technology Group and DARPA. He is the recipient of DARPA's prestigious Game Changer Award (2023).
