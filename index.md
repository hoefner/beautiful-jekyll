I am a computer scientist at the [Australian National University](https://cecs.anu.edu.au), working at the intersection of formal methods, programming languages, and concurrency. My research transforms how software systems are built — using the certainty of mathematical proof to guarantee their correctness and security.

My work addresses real-world challenges: I led the first rigorous formal analysis of the AODV routing protocol, used by emergency services worldwide, uncovering critical flaws that contradicted the official standard. As part of DARPA's High-Assurance Cyber Military Systems programme, my team designed and formally verified protocols now deployed in operational unmanned aircraft — work recognised with DARPA's prestigious [Game Changer Award](https://www.darpa.mil/news-events/2023-01-24) in 2023.

As Associate Director of Education in the School of Computing, I lead the design and transformation of curricula across eight computing programs, supporting over 6,000 student enrolments per semester.
